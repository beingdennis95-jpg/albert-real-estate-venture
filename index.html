Video Instructions

Simple Video Guidelines for Better Property Tours.

A good property video does not need to feel complicated. It needs to be clear, steady, well-lit, and easy for buyers to follow.

01

Start With the Front Exterior

Begin with a clean shot of the front of the property. Hold the camera steady for 3–5 seconds before moving.

02

Walk Slowly Through the Main Spaces

Move through the entry, living room, kitchen, bedrooms, bathrooms, backyard, and any standout features. Avoid fast camera movements.

03

Use Natural Light

Open curtains, turn on lights, and record during the brightest part of the day when possible. Bright spaces feel more inviting online.

04

Record Horizontal and Vertical Versions

Horizontal videos work well for websites and YouTube. Vertical clips work better for Instagram Reels, TikTok, YouTube Shorts, and mobile ads.

05

Keep It Clean and Quiet

Remove clutter, close toilet lids, clear counters, avoid background noise, and make sure each room looks ready before recording.

06

Highlight Lifestyle Features

Show the backyard, view, natural light, storage, garage, upgrades, nearby parks, and anything that makes the property more desirable.
